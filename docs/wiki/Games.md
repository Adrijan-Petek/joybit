### How to Play
1. **Connect Wallet**: Ensure your wallet is connected and has sufficient ETH
2. **Select Level**: Choose your starting level (higher levels = higher rewards)
3. **Match Tiles**: Click and drag to swap adjacent tiles
4. **Reach Target**: Match tiles to accumulate the required score
5. **Claim Rewards**: Win JOYB tokens based on your performance

- **3 tiles**: 30 points
- **4 tiles**: 80 points (2.67√ó multiplier)
- **5 tiles**: 150 points (5√ó multiplier)

## üÉè 3-Card Game

### How to Play
1. **Place Bet**: Pay 0.002 ETH entry fee
2. **Choose Card**: Select 1 of 3 face-down cards
3. **Reveal Result**: Blockchain determines the winner randomly
4. **Claim Winnings**: Win 0.006 JOYB if you choose correctly

### Game Mechanics
- **True Randomness**: Uses blockchain block hash for fair results
- **33.3% Win Rate**: Each card has equal probability
- **Instant Results**: No waiting - results are immediate
- **Fair Distribution**: Winners receive 3x their entry fee in JOYB

- **Reward Tracking**: Monitor wins vs losses over time
